## Reflection

The claim that looked most authoritative but was actually weakest was Claim #2: "The median placement package at IITs in 2025 was ₹18.5 LPA."

Gemini presented the statistic confidently and attached a reputable source name, which made it appear trustworthy. Perplexity initially reinforced the claim by citing related placement discussions. However, when I opened the primary source, I could not find any IIT-wide median package statistic. The source discussed employability and hiring trends but not the claimed figure.

This demonstrated that a confident tone and a recognizable organization do not guarantee correctness. The most important lesson from this lab is that AI-generated statistics must always be verified against the original source before being shared with students or included in educational material.
